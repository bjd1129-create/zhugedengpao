## 摘要

The Model Context Protocol (MCP) is an open standard that gives AI models a universal way to connect to external tools, data sources, and services. Adopted by OpenAI, Google DeepMind, Microsoft, and thousands of development teams.

## 核心要点

- MCP 是 AI 模型连接外部工具和数据源的开放标准
- 被 OpenAI、Google DeepMind、Microsoft 等主要厂商采用
- Python 和 TypeScript SDK 使用量持续增长
- 成为 AI 连接真实世界的实际标准（de facto standard）
